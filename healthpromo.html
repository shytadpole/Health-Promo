Health Promotion and Disease Prevention

Nurses contribute greatly to the health of clients and population groups using health promotion and disease prevention strategies. Nursing care of the client incorporates knowledge of early detection of disease and actions to promote optimal health.

Health Promotion

Includes client education, health risk assessment, wellness assessment, lifestyle and behavior changes, and environmental control programs
